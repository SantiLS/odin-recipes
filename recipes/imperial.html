Imperial Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs/li>
  • 60 grams of sugar
  • 60 grams of all-purpose flour (0000 flour)
  • 1 liter of heavy cream
  • 100 grams of sugar
  • 200 grams of almonds
  • 200 grams of meringues
  • Pastry dulce de leche

Steps

  1. We place the flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk, and mix. Then, we add the beaten egg.
  2. Knead the dough and add the butter. When you have a smooth dough ball, place it in a container with flour, cover it, and let it rest until it doubles in size.
  3. Deflate the dough and place it in the mold, using a ring.
  4. Let it rest for another 30 minutes, then make some small holes all over the dough. Cover it with plastic wrap and preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  5. Heat the heavy cream and pour it over the cake. Sprinkle with powdered sugar, and it's ready to serve.
